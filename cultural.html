Executive Summary

A young girl stands on a giant worm, receiving all the applauses from the residents in the valley. It is the last scenery of “Nausicaa of the Valley of the Wind”. It a animation that tells a story about the princess of the valley fight towards the giant worms army alone, because her father was dead. She meant to be the hero of the Valley. That leads to the question of since when women have the consciousness to fight with men or for themselves? In the really society, go through the past five decades, women’s social status have been improved surprisingly as they can even be take the leadership of a country. In this essay, it will focus on from what aspect they have been improved and how they achieve that. To summary, there are three main aspects: Education level, labor force or work, and political status. Moreover, the whole world will continue making good efforts on the social equality.
