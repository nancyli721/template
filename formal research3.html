3 processes of product positioning

A scholar journal named "Product positioning based on search, experience and credence attributes using conjoint analysis" (Arora, 2006) mainly discussed the strategies of how to position a particular project. The scholar use teeth whitening as an overwhelmed example to interpret the cores of product positioning.

Here is the basic abstract of the scholar journal. "The study uses conjoint analysis to estimate the utilities of key attributes of teeth-whitening products." (Arora, 2006) through the processes of investigation, survey and the design, based on desirable attributes of the product, the product could be priced reasonably.

According to the essay, product positioning is mainly based on 3parts: estimating the utilities of various attributes, determining the pricing method, last but not least, exposing both impacts could be on customers as well as the side- effects.
