Conclusion

In conclusion, Product positioning is a marketing technique intended to present products in the best possible light to diverse target audiences. If a company tends to sell additional products, they will need to have the overview of the potential market. After determining the potential consumers, the functions and properties of the product could be decided. Last but not least, a good overwhelmed survey about the market is essential for determining the price. The company may want to have the slightly lower price that can attract more customers and earn more money to take over the market. Through the processes of investigation, survey and the design, based on desirable attributes of the product, the product could be priced reasonably.
