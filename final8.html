    Conclusion

    All in short, in order to have a regretless participation and satisfied grade in WRA 110 class, “never be a last minute person” is the key quote you have to remember. Though the first step is always the hardest, follow the instructions and learned by heart can beat any difficulties. The class is not always grading on your writing skills, so do not be too worry to finish the essays like finish a mission, with the feeling of awe. Overall, in the preparation stage, consider on your theme thoroughly first. After that, you should use logical or a specific sequence to indicate the theme as and evidences. When facing the group project, don’t be afraid of communication and negotiation. A well-organized team is more likely to produce fascinating movies. Attitude is everything. Hope you guys enjoy this article and enjoy your life. Go Green, Go white!
