Executive Summary

In this formal essay, it is going to explain the process and the importance of product positioning before released through several specific examples. From any market model, one of the same information can be told that each product needed to be well-assessed and organized. Every aspect of the product should be though thoroughly once more to ensure it can work well as producers were planed. This topic would be expanded into mainly two parts. One of them is deeply explaining how to position a product reasonably that do help selling the product and match the original intention. One the other hand, through the explanation, several models will show up to help the explanation. Basically, when facing diversity of consumers and the giant world area, you need a good strategy to sell your product or, in other words, to stand out. To summarize, firming the target, analyzing the potential market, shaping the brand, defined the competitors, and using marketing strategies.
